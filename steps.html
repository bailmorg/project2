Steps 1-3

Step 1.

  • Clean off raw chicken and cut it up into small pieces
  • Meanwhile, measure out 1/2 cup of rice and rinse in water until it is no longer cloudy

    • Step 2.

  • In a bowl, mix garlic, paprika, salt, pepper, and chicken seasoning
  • Preheat rice cooker and add rinsed off rice. Add 3/4 cup of coconut milk

    • Step 3.

  • Mix chicken in bowl of seasoning and completely cover each piece
  • Stir rice and coconut milk and place mixture into rice cooker. Set for 20 min
